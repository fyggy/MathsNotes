Let $X$ be a set, and let $C(X)$ be the free vector space on $X {}$ over ${} \Gamma {}$. Let $F {}$ be another vector space over $\Gamma {}$, and let ${} f:X\to{}F {}$.

Let ${} \iota:X\to{}C(X) {}$ be defined by ${} x\mapsto f_{x} {}$ is the inclusion map. Then ${} \iota(X) {}$ is a basis of $C(X)$. Then ${} \iota^{0}:X\to{}\iota(X) {}$ is a bijection. Therefore, there exists a unique linear extension of ${} \varphi^{0}$, ${} \varphi:C(X)\to{}F {}$.
