4.3.10:
We find ${} x,\, y {}$ such that
$$
\nabla f(x,\, y)=\begin{pmatrix} 2x+2y \\ 2x-2y+3 \end{pmatrix} =\begin{pmatrix} 0 \\ 0 \end{pmatrix} 
$$
So ${} x=-y {}$, and ${} 4x+3=0 {}$, ${} x=-3 /4 {}$ and ${} y=3 /4 {}$. Now
$$
\begin{align}
 f_{xx}(x,\, y) & =2   \\
f_{xy}(x,\, y) & =2 \\
f_{yy}(x,\, y) & =-2
 \end{align}
$$
so the determinant of the hessian is ${} 2\cdot (-2)-2^{2}=-8 {}$
