### Definition 1.1: Power Series
A power series is a series of the form
$$
f(z)=\sum_{n=0}^{\infty} a_{n}(z-z_{0})^{n}
$$
where ${} z_{0} \in \mathbb{C} {}$ and ${} \{ a_{n} \}_{n=0}^{\infty} \subseteq \mathbb{C} {}$ are called the coefficients
