# Greatest common devisor
### Definition 1.1: GCD
Let ${} a,\, b \in \mathbb{Z} {}$. We say that $a {}$ divides $b {}$, and write ${} a \mid b {}$ iff there exists some ${} d \in \mathbb{Z} {}$ such that ${} ad=b {}$.
#### Example:
${} 11 {}$ divides ${} 143 {}$
