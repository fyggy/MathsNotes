a)
$$
\begin{align}
359 & =2 \cdot  133 +93 \\
133 & =1 \cdot 93 +40 \\
93 & =2\cdot 40+13 \\
40 & =3\cdot 13+1 \\
13 & =13\cdot 1
\end{align}
$$
Therefore ${} \gcd(359,\, 133)=1 {}$. Now
$$
\begin{align}
 1 & =40-3 \cdot 13 \\
 & =40-3\cdot (93-2\cdot 40)  \\
 & =7\cdot 40 - 3 \cdot 93 \\
 & =7\cdot (133-1\cdot 93)-3\cdot 93 \\
 & =7\cdot 133-10\cdot 93 \\
 & =7\cdot 133-10\cdot (359-2\cdot 133) \\
 & =27\cdot 133-10\cdot 359
 \end{align}
$$
